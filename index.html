SEC-01 / PROFILE

Built on Precision.
Driven by Detail.

I am Kareem Ahmed Tantawy, a Civil Engineer with focused expertise across three interlocking disciplines: structural design, shop drawing production, and BIM modeling. My work doesn't stop at calculations — it extends through every drawing, every coordination clash, every detail that makes a structure buildable.

I've contributed to structurally demanding projects including military facilities requiring strict compliance, high-rise residential towers with complex lateral load systems, luxury villas, and projects across the UAE & KSA where international standards and delivery precision are non-negotiable.

Whether I'm running a finite element model in ETABS, coordinating discipline clashes in Revit, or finalizing reinforcement layouts in ASD — my goal is always the same: drawings that build correctly, the first time.

"Precision in calculation. Clarity in documentation. Safety in execution."
